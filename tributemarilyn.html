About Marilyn Monroe

Marilyn Monroe was an American actress, model, and singer, and one of the most iconic figures of the 20th century.

She became famous for playing comedic “blonde bombshell” roles in Hollywood films during the 1950s.

Her most famous movies include Gentlemen Prefer Blondes, Some Like It Hot, and The Seven Year Itch.

Marilyn Monroe was admired for her beauty, charm, and screen presence, becoming a global cultural icon.

Despite her fame, she faced personal struggles throughout her life.

She passed away in 1962, but remains one of the most enduring legends in film history.

Marilyn Monroe didn’t wear fashion to be seen — she wore it to be remembered.
